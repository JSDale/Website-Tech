Hair Care Tips

Rules of Thumb

Washing:

  • Shampoo scalp only, if not it will dry out ends.
  • Be gentle when washing. Don’t use fingernails or harsh rubbing as it can damage your scalp and cause tangles.
  • Condition the ends of your hair only, if put on scalp it can cause build-up of sebum.
  • Use a microfibre towel, ‘normal’ towels catch hair fibres which causes frizz.
  • Never rub hair with towel, this cause hair damage; hair loss and causes frizz.

Daily:

  • Use a wooden/boar bristle brush, as this distributes hair oils throughout hair and prevents static.
  • Put hair in protective hair styles, this protects against enivromental damage.
  • When putting hair up, use invisible scrunchies or hair sticks
  • Use a silk pillowcase, this protects hair overnight as it prevents friction which induces stress on hair fibres and causes frizz.
